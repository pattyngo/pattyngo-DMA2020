Sammy Spartan

This is my take on our school mascot. I had an assignment to do an installation piece in the art building with instruction of not taking up too much space. I saw this as a challenge and wanted to create somethning that was "there but not really there" by creating an AR model. I chose Snapchat as my platform becuase most students already have Snapchat on their phones. Within one week my Snapchat filter was used over 400 times.
